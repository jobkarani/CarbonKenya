About RafikiAfrica

Promotion of quality, equity, and marketing of differentiated African carbon credits to both African and global buyers across African markets.

Our Mission

To provide access to both offsetters and emitters and achieve carbon neutrality. We are committed to helping our clients take a proactive approach to reducing their carbon footprint and making a positive impact on the environment.

Our Values

Transparency by default

We believe in providing clear and accurate information to our clients to help them make informed decisions.

Collaboration and partnership

We believe in working closely with our clients to develop customized solutions that meet their unique needs and goals.

Innovation

We are constantly seeking new and innovative ways to reduce carbon emissions and promote sustainable practices.
