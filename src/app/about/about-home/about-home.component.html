About RafikiCarbon

Promotion of quality, equity, and marketing of differentiated African carbon credits to both African and global carbon credit buyers.

Our Mission

To provide adequate and reliable market access to African project offsetters and emitters with purpose to achieve carbon neutrality. We are committed to helping our clients take a proactive approach to reducing their carbon footprint and making a positive impact on the environment especially from African projects!
